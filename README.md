# Healthcare-Marketing-Campaign-Data-Analysis
-->Conducted marketing analytics on a healthcare outreach dataset to evaluate the effectiveness of marketing campaigns targeted at physicians across multiple communication channels.
-->Performed data exploration and preprocessing using Pandas, analyzing fields such as Communication Source, Channel, Therapeutic Area, Device, Activity, Activity_Count, Date, and Physician ID.
-->Calculated key marketing performance metrics including Reach (unique physicians contacted), Frequency (average number of interactions per physician), and Engagement (total physician interactions with campaigns).
-->Implemented segmented analysis by Communication Source, Channel, and Month, enabling comparison of marketing performance across different campaign channels and time periods.
-->Identified trends and patterns in physician engagement, highlighting high-performing marketing channels and outreach strategies using data-driven analysis.
-->Developed visual dashboards and analytical reports with charts and tables to communicate insights and provide strategic recommendations for optimizing healthcare marketing campaigns.
**Dataset Used**
Marketing Data - Hiring.xlsx – Dataset containing healthcare marketing interactions with physicians including communication source, marketing channels, therapeutic area focus, device information, activity type, interaction count, date, and physician identifiers.
**Technologies used**: Python, Pandas, NumPy, Jupyter Notebook, Data Visualization (Matplotlib / Seaborn / Excel), Data Analytics
